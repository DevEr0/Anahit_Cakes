Our Story

Sweetness, crafted
since 1994.

For three decades, Anahit Cakes has been a small temple to the sweet things in life on Mantashyan Street in Yerevan. What began as a single confectioner's passion has grown into a beloved patisserie known for tender layers, careful detail, and recipes refined day after day.

The Craft

How a cake becomes
a Anahit cake.

The Recipe

Every cake begins on paper. We sketch the layers, balance the sweetness, and choose ingredients that honor Armenian tradition — local honey, walnuts, rose, fresh dairy.

1

The Foundation

Sponge layers baked the same morning — never the day before. Eggs cracked one at a time, flour sifted twice, batter whipped until it ribbons off the whisk.

2

The Filling

Creams whipped to a soft peak. Fruit reduced into glossy compotes. Each layer brushed with a touch of syrup so the sponge stays tender, never dry.

3

The Decoration

By hand, always. Gold leaf laid with a brush, sugar roses piped petal by petal, gilded edges traced with a steady wrist. Sweet things deserve patience.

4

The Box

Carefully into our signature box, with a ribbon and a name. Cakes leave Mantashyan 41 as fresh as they were made. Delivered in Yerevan or picked up that same day.
